Elevator Pitch App Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes the treatment of information provided or collected on applications we make available on third-party sites or platforms if disclosed to you in connection with use of the application. We follow this privacy policy in accordance with local law in the places where we operate.

No personal information is collected

The Elevator Pitch app does not collect any personal information about the user and does not use any third party software for analytics or any other purposes. The apps only store your app settings/preferences/photos/files on your device.

No ads

The Elevator Pitch app does not have any ads.

No Internet connection required

The Elevator Pitch app does not require an internet connection to function. The app will need internet access for in app purchases and restoring purchases previously made at Apple’s App store. An internet connection will be required if the user chhoses to upload the video to social websties like Facebook and Youtube

In-app purchases

The Elevator Pitch app uses an in-app purchase in the iOS app for the video export to work.

No push notifications

The Elevator Pitch app does not have push notifications.

Integration with Social Sites

The Elevator Pitch app can optionally let you upload videos to social sites like Facebook and Youtube. The app does not make any other posts to the user's accounts except the videos that the user has chosen to upload.
